## Lunch/Dinner

### Deconstructed Cabbage Rolls

**Ingredients**

- 1 lb ground chicken or turkey
- Minced garlic
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 bag coleslaw mix
- 1 can tomato paste
- 1/2 carton chicken broth
- 1 cup basmati rice

**Prep**

1. Brown meat in a bit of oil.
2. Add everything else except coleslaw mix.
3. Cover and cook over low heat for 20 minutes.
4. Add coleslaw mix, stir, and cook 5 more minutes.

### Cheddar Chicken Biscuit

**Ingredients**

- 1 chicken, shredded
- 1 bag shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 stick butter
- 1 box cheddar herb biscuit mix
- 1 cup milk
- 4 tbsp butter

**Prep**

1. Melt 1/2 stick of butter in a metal pan in the oven at 400°F.
2. Once melted, remove from oven.
3. Add shredded chicken on top, plus any seasoning you like.
4. Add a bag of frozen vegetables if you like.
5. Make biscuit mix according to box instructions.
6. Place biscuit mix on top of chicken.
7. Melt 1/2 stick butter and pour on top of biscuit.
8. Bake at 400°F for about 20 minutes.

### Creamy Chicken w/ Veggies

**Ingredients**

- 2 chicken breasts, thin sliced and cut into chunks if possible
- 1/2 bottle Caesar dressing (Bolthouse low-fat recommended)
- 1 small bag mini potatoes, cut in half
- 1 bag pre-cut fresh broccoli
- Olive oil, salt, pepper, and seasonings to taste

**Prep**

1. Put cut veggies on a cookie tray.
2. Drizzle with oil and seasonings; mix around.
3. In a separate bowl, mix dressing with chicken until coated.
4. Put chicken on tray with veggies.
5. Drizzle leftover dressing on veggies.
6. Bake at 350°F for 25-30 minutes, depending on chicken thickness.

### Stuffed Peppers

**Ingredients**

- Cooked ground turkey
- 1 cup cooked rice
- 3 tbsp or small can tomato paste
- 1/2 cup water
- Garlic salt, Italian seasoning, and pepper to taste
- 2 green or red peppers
- Mozzarella cheese, shredded

**Prep**

1. Cut peppers in half and clean out seeds.
2. Mix all other ingredients except cheese.
3. Stuff pepper halves with mixture and put in a baking dish.
4. Bake covered at 350°F for 35-45 minutes.

### Italian Sliders

**Ingredients**

- 1 pack King’s Hawaiian Sweet Slider Rolls (orange pack)
- 6 slices pepper jack cheese
- 6 slices provolone or mozzarella cheese
- 9-12 slices salami
- 9-12 slices pepperoni (large deli cut)
- 8 slices large deli ham
- Tomato slices
- 2 tbsp butter + 1 tsp parsley + 1 tsp garlic powder (for brushing roll tops)
- Optional toppings: tomato, red onion, pickles, peperoncini

**Salad for Sandwich**

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 bag thinly shredded iceberg lettuce
- Optional: sliced red onions, banana peppers, or red peppers

**Prep**

1. Slice and prepare rolls.
2. Layer with pepper jack, salami, ham, pepperoni, and provolone.
3. Brush tops with butter, parsley, and garlic powder.
4. Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes.
5. Mix mayo, red wine vinegar, olive oil, Italian seasoning, and oregano.
6. Whisk, then add lettuce and toss.
7. Place salad on cooked sandwiches and serve.

### Broccoli Cheddar Soup

**Ingredients**

- See ingredient photo:

![CHICKEN](Attachments/3A8EFBAC-B537-4C88-8546-9FB65DDEAB40.heic)

**Prep**

1. In a pot over medium heat, add olive oil and butter.
2. Once melted, add onions and cook 3-4 minutes until softened.
3. Lower heat to low and sprinkle in flour.
4. Stir to form a roux and cook 2-3 minutes.
5. Add chicken broth, bouillon powder, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
6. Stir, then add broccoli and carrots.
7. Bring to a boil, stirring occasionally, and cook 10-15 minutes.
8. Turn off heat and rest 2-3 minutes.
9. Stir in heavy whipping cream.
10. Add cheese a little at a time, stirring until melted and smooth.
11. Serve and enjoy.

### Crack Chicken Casserole

**Ingredients**

- 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded/chunked
- 1/2 lb pasta (elbows or penne)
- 1/2 lb bacon, chopped
- 3 tbsp butter
- 1/2 onion, chopped
- 1 can cream of chicken soup
- 16 oz sour cream
- 4 oz cream cheese, softened
- 1 packet ranch seasoning
- 1/3 cup half and half
- 2 cups shredded cheddar
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Onion powder to taste
- Sliced scallions

**Prep**

1. Cook bacon until crispy; remove.
2. Wipe skillet, leaving about 1 tsp bacon grease.
3. Add butter, then onion; cook on low until softened.
4. In a large bowl, combine soup, sour cream, cream cheese, half and half, and ranch.
5. Season with salt, pepper, and onion powder.
6. Cook and drain pasta; add to bowl.
7. Add chicken, sautéed onions, and 1 cup cheese; stir well.
8. Pour into prepared 9x13 baking dish and spread evenly.
9. Top with remaining cheese and cooked bacon.
10. Bake at 375°F for 25-30 minutes.
11. Remove and sprinkle with scallions.

## Desserts

### Cinnamon Roll Sliders

**Ingredients**

- 1 package (12-count) King’s Hawaiian Original Sweet Rolls
- 1/2 cup butter, softened
- 1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
- 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
- 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened (icing)
- 1 cup powdered sugar (icing)
- 1-2 tbsp milk (icing)

**Prep**

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Cut sheet of Hawaiian rolls in half horizontally to create top and bottom slabs.
3. Mix softened butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon.
4. Spread half the mixture on bottom slab, then place top slab over it.
5. Spread remaining butter mixture on top.
6. Bake 12-15 minutes until golden brown.
7. Whisk cream cheese, powdered sugar, and milk until smooth.
8. Drizzle icing over warm rolls.
